6.7 Tier VII Tier stats

Tier VII stats

This page list statistics of battles fought at tier VII during update 6.7.

Battles per Player

Number of battles fought per player during the update 6.7.

On average, players played 86 battles. Median was 41 and the largest number of battles recorded by a single player (in the dataset) during the update 6.7 was 3 843 battles. The following tables shows battle counts by N% most inactive players during the update.

Battles per Player table
