6.0 Tier VI Tier stats

Tier VI stats

This page list statistics of battles fought at tier VI during update 6.0.

Battles per player

Number of battles fought per player during the update 6.0.

On average, players played 74 battles. Median was 33 and the largest number of battles recorded by a single player (in the dataset) during the update 6.0 was 11808 battles. The following tables shows battle counts by N% most inactive players during the update.
