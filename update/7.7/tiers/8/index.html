7.7 Tier VIII Tier stats

Tier VIII stats

This page list statistics of battles fought at tier VIII during update 7.7.

Battles per Player

Number of battles fought per player during the update 7.7.

On average, players played 145 battles. Median was 74 and the largest number of battles recorded by a single player (in the dataset) during the update 7.7 was 13 929 battles. The following tables shows battle counts by N% most inactive players during the update.
