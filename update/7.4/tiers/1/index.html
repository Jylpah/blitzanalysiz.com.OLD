7.4 Tier I Tier stats

Tier I stats

Preliminary stats

This page list statistics of battles fought at tier I during update 7.4.

Battles per Player

Number of battles fought per player during the update 7.4.

On average, players played 9 battles. Median was 3 and the largest number of battles recorded by a single player (in the dataset) during the update 7.4 was 1 468 battles. The following tables shows battle counts by N% most inactive players during the update.
